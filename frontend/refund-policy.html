Refund Policy

Last updated: December 25, 2024

Our Commitment

At GB Dry Fruits, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with our products, we're here to help make things right.

Eligibility for Refunds

Quality Issues

We offer full refunds or replacements for products with quality issues such as:

  • Mold or spoilage
  • Foreign objects in packaging
  • Incorrect product received
  • Damaged packaging affecting product quality

Delivery Issues

Refunds are available for:

  • Orders not delivered within 10 business days (Pakistan) / 30 days (International)
  • Products damaged during shipping
  • Missing items from your order

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Products returned after 7 days of delivery
  • Products with opened/used packaging (unless quality issue)
  • Personal preference or taste dissatisfaction
  • Products damaged due to improper storage by customer

Refund Process

Step 1: Contact Us

Within 7 days of receiving your order, contact us with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos of the issue
  • Description of the problem

Step 2: Review

Our team will review your claim within 24-48 hours and may request additional information if needed.

Step 3: Resolution

Based on the issue, we'll offer:

  • Full refund to original payment method
  • Product replacement (including shipping)
  • Store credit for future purchases

Refund Timeline

Payment Method Refunds

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 3-5 business days
  • JazzCash/EasyPaisa: 2-3 business days

Cash on Delivery

Refunds for COD orders will be processed via bank transfer or JazzCash/EasyPaisa.

Return Shipping

For quality issues, we cover return shipping costs. For other approved returns, customer may be responsible for return shipping.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for:

  • Different products of equal or lesser value
  • Same product with different packaging/weight
  • Exchanges must be requested within 7 days of delivery

Special Circumstances

Natural Disasters

If shipping delays occur due to natural disasters, we'll offer order cancellation with full refund or reshipment when possible.

Customs Issues

For international orders held by customs, we'll work with you to resolve issues but cannot guarantee release of products.
